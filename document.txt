Anthropic AI Assistant — Help Center

Refund Policy
We offer a 30 day money back guarantee on all plans. To request a refund contact support@company.com with your order ID. Refunds are processed within 7 business days.

Pricing Plans
Basic plan costs $9 per month and includes 100 queries per day.
Pro plan costs $29 per month and includes unlimited queries and priority support.
Enterprise plan is custom priced and includes dedicated support and SLA guarantees.

Supported Features
The assistant supports text generation, summarization, question answering, and code generation.
It does not support image generation or voice input at this time.

Data Privacy
All conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest.
We do not share user data with third parties.
Data is retained for 30 days by default and can be deleted on request.

Technical Requirements
The API requires Node.js version 18 or higher.
Rate limits are 100 requests per minute on Basic and unlimited on Pro.
Maximum input size is 4000 tokens per request.
